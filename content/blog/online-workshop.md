To participate in our 1 hour online workshop, [register here](https://songfarmer.bandcamp.com/merch/songfarmer-workshop-online-registration) and, via email, you will receive a private link to the session. In the session, Gordy and Owen will introduce the principles and processes of Songfarmer and offer clear next steps for developing songwriting skills. After the session, video of the workshop will be available for download. Registration for the online session is $50 and is available [here](https://songfarmer.bandcamp.com/merch/songfarmer-workshop-online-registration).
